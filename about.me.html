Aimer La Vie!

[Love Life]

I created this website as an outlet and safespace not only for my art but the art I am beautifully surrounded with. Being at The New School in NYC, is an eye opening adventure, pervaded with unique personalities, talents, and styles that I want to highlight & expose. Our generation has picked up and reshaped the idea of what we consider “avant garde” fashion today, transforming it to be present in all aspects of our society and culture. The artists featured are just some of the great things this city has to offer. Enjoy the ride, for life is beautiful!
