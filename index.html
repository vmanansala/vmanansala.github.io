She is beauty

Casting down her rays on the people

Holding up the universe

That she calls her own

A symbol of strength.

I praise thee who holds the universe I live in together

She reigns over all the other stars

And for that she is almighty, beautiful, and strong-

All in one.

Blazing her rays on life;

She is powerful

She is the reason why we eat, breathe, and live

Without her we are nothing

She controls the start of each day

And controls the beginning of each night

But apart from all the beauty She is ruthless holding rage within her Hoping no one will come too closely to her

But, she is beauty.

Holding up everything that revolves around her

Appearing every morning

And disappearing every night

I am her

She is me

Making daily appearances

Filled with heat and beauty

That can attract or push one away

I am a fire sensation

Getting to close to me can harm you

For I have tendencies to hurt someone

Filled with nothing but fire and insecurities

I am her and she is me

I am nothing but merely a star playing my role in the universe

Hoping no one gets too close to me

And leaves
